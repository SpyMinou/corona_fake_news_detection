You Can Submit A News Article Or A Post You've Found On Social Media And Find Out If It's Real Or Misinformation

Why COVID-19 misinformation Matters

Misinformation about COVID-19 can have serious consequences, Inaccurate information about the virus, its transmission, prevention, and treatment can lead to confusion, fear, and misguided actions. It is important to combat COVID-19 fake news to ensure accurate information reaches the public. Our mission is to help you distinguish between real and fake information.

What is COVID-19?

COVID-19, also known as the coronavirus disease 2019, is a viral illness caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. It emerged in late 2019, spreading globally and causing a pandemic. Symptoms include fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. It spreads through respiratory droplets and close contact. To combat COVID-19, follow guidelines, practice good hand hygiene, wear masks, and maintain physical distancing.

how did we make this possible

Our project aimed to fight against fake news specifically related to COVID-19 by developing a detection system. using advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning, we trained a model that can analyze various sources of information and identify COVID-19 misinformation accurately. With this project, our primary objective was to contribute to the global efforts in combating COVID-19 misinformation by providing a tool that helps individuals to distinguish between real and fake news online.
